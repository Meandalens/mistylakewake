About Us

Misty Lake Wake was set up by Abe Sapien and Amphibian Man, both mermen, after they left the film industry. Having met on film sets of the Mexican director Guillermo del Toro ( Gómez ) and becoming good friends over their years in the film industry, with both sharing a passion for water and the many water based sports, in particular wakeboarding and more specifically cable riding after visiting some of the best cable parks around the world. After taking part in these sports in their spare time and basically at every possible free moment to hone the skills and nail down their respective styles, they decided to open up their own cable park in Mallow, County Cork in the south west of Ireland after discovering Misty Lakes and its unique properties.

With its unique setting as well as set-up Misty Lake Wake is the perfect spot to both try wakeboarding for the first time as well as being the perfect spot for progression and improving your riding. From your first time on the wake to learning your first tricks and hitting your first obstacles the instructors will have you falling in love with the sport and eager to progress with the aim to have you competition ready, all in your first season. All the instructors, all locals, have a passion for the sport and will pass that passion onto you.

Cable Packages

4 week course of with 3 sets per week with the aim of getting you from absolute novice to hitting your first kickers and rails with you riding away as well as your first rotations/spins (All Equipment is included).

Full Gold

For the entusiastic riders out there - €10 Peak Sets €6 Off peak sets No limit on amounts of sets per day Can book 4 sets in advance Free weekly buddy pass Gear hire and events extra.
